Rota

Create bespoke intervention rotas in minutes.

The Rota tool builds a clear intervention timetable that works around your class schedule and available support. Upload your timetable, set staff availability, and the tool suggests a fair, practical plan that maximises support without disrupting core lessons. Everything is anonymised automatically, and you can adjust the plan with a simple drag-and-drop grid. No more spending hours juggling who goes where — the rota is done in minutes and ready to use.

Provision Mapper

Map needs and provision quickly.

The Provision Mapper helps you keep track of every intervention and support plan in one simple grid. You can log needs, map provision across the week, and quickly see who’s getting what support — without drowning in paperwork. The tool automatically links back to your central record, so updates flow through to reports and planning. It’s built to make SEND tracking easier, clearer, and more transparent, saving you time and giving you a big-picture view at a glance.

Reports

Draft clear reports from your notes and data.

The Reports tool turns your notes and pupil data into clear, professional-sounding report drafts in minutes. You can select the sections you want, highlight key points from your own records, and let the tool do the heavy lifting of phrasing and structuring. Everything stays anonymised and under your control — you review, edit, and approve before anything is finalised. It’s designed to save you hours of repetitive writing while keeping your reports personal and accurate.
