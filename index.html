The Tools

Groups (Prototype)

Create and manage pupil groups easily.

The Groups tool shows how ChalkboardAI will help teachers quickly create and adapt pupil groups for interventions, projects, and targeted support. In this prototype you can upload a sample class list, anonymise it instantly, and test out the early grouping logic.
Important: This version is for demonstration only.
Please do not use real pupil information yet — the secure, fully-featured version is still in development.

Rota

Create bespoke intervention rotas in minutes.

The Rota tool builds a clear, workable intervention timetable around your class schedule and support. In the full version, you’ll upload your timetable, tag support, and generate a complete rota automatically. This prototype lets you load a sample timetable and see how the anonymised rota is produced. As with all ChalkboardAI tools, everything is anonymised before it reaches the AI.
No more juggling interventions — build a rota in minutes.
Click this panel to test the tool!

Provision Mapper

Map needs and provision quickly.

The Provision Mapper helps you keep track of every intervention and support plan in one simple grid. You can log needs, map provision across the week, and quickly see who’s getting what support — without drowning in paperwork. The tool automatically links back to your central record, so updates flow through to reports and planning. It’s built to make SEND tracking easier, clearer, and more transparent, saving you time and giving you a big-picture view at a glance.
Click this panel to test the tool!

Reports

Draft clear reports from your notes and data.

The Reports tool turns your notes and pupil data into clear, professional-sounding report drafts in minutes. You can select the sections you want, highlight key points from your own records, and let the tool do the heavy lifting of phrasing and structuring. Everything stays anonymised and under your control — you review, edit, and approve before anything is finalised. It’s designed to save you hours of repetitive writing while keeping your reports personal and accurate.
Click this panel to test the tool!
All tools are free to use. No sign-up required. No data is stored or shared. All processing is done locally in your browser.
